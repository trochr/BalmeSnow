BalmeSnow — Webcam Viewer

Quick Help

  • Magnifier: Use your mouse wheel (scroll) to zoom in a circular magnifier. Scroll up to zoom in, scroll down to zoom out. When zoom is 1× the magnifier hides.
  • Next / Previous: Click the ◀ and ▶ buttons or use the left/right arrow keys on your keyboard to move one image back or forward. The magnifier stays where you placed it while you move between images — handy for spotting small changes in snow level.
  • Jump by time: Hold Shift and press the left or right arrow keys to jump to the nearest morning/noon or similar natural break (handy to skip through time). To jump by whole days instead, hold Shift + Control while using the arrow keys.
